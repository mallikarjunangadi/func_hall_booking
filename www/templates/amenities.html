Amenities

DIGITAL STAGE

A large format LED digital backdrop sets the stage for all your important events & occasions. The LED screens are also built with powerful KARAOKE systems. The play of light and sound will ensure that there is never a dull moment and guests are entertained all through the function. By going digital and choosing technology over tradition, you can say goodbye to boring stage backdrops once and for all!

AUDIO-VISUAL

The contemporary audio-visual system is one of the key differentiators at the TSR Conference Hall. The built-in ambient lights are equipped to change the ambience to align with your corporate identity hue. You can plug & play your Power Point presentation or audio-video content simply by using a pen drive into the USB/HDMI port. The process is not only hassle-free but you also get to save thousands of Malaysian Ringgits by doing away with the need for outsourcing external audio-visual equipment.

CHANGING ROOMS

There are exclusive changing rooms for both the ladies and gentlemen. The backstage is professionally and cleverly designed to demarcate the two changing areas. Needless to add, separate washrooms are also available
